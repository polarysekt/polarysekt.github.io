## From the archives:

It's often useful to indicate that something is a trademark (registered even) and/or has copyrights reserved. 
In times past it was common to use (c),(C) or (r),(R) to represent the symbol.

Copyright: ©
Trademark: ™
Servicesmark: ℠
Registered Trademark: ®
Ampersand: &
